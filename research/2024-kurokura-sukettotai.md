### 概要

### SnowNotesにおける位置づけ

豪雪地域では、雪は以下を変化させる。

- 労働の形
- 集落の協力構造
- 高齢化との交差による持続性
- 地域の社会制度

本事例は、
「雪 → 人間の行動 → 社会構造」
という連鎖の具体例である。

## English Version

### Overview

This note documents field experience with the Kurokura Mutual Aid Snow Removal Team in Matsunoyama, Tokamachi City, Niigata Prefecture.

The observations are based on direct participation as a regional revitalization officer and analyze the operational structure, challenges, and long-term sustainability of the snow removal organization.

This document reframes the report as part of the SnowNotes research system.

---

### Position within SnowNotes

Snow is not merely a meteorological phenomenon.

In heavy snow regions, snow reshapes:

- Labor systems
- Community cooperation structures
- Sustainability under demographic aging
- Local institutional arrangements

This case illustrates the chain:

Snow → Human Action → Social Structure
